BALD EAGLE

Haliaeetus leucocephalus

I soar above the reaching hills, on lifting winds I fly. My prey I seek with piercing eye, grasp with talon strong. By strength of wing will soar on high.
Most of the more than 60 species occur in Eurasia and Africa. Outside this area, just two species can be found in the United States and Canada, nine more in Central and South America, and three in Australia
